About the Book

*The Monk Who Sold His Ferrari* by Robin Sharma is a spiritual fable about a high-powered lawyer, Julian Mantle, who chooses a radically different path to discover true happiness and purpose in life.

Story Backdrop

After a sudden heart attack, Julian embarks on a soul-searching journey to the Himalayas, leaving behind his luxury, law career, and Ferrari. There, he meets sages who teach him ancient techniques for a more joyful, purposeful life.

Key Life Lessons

  • Master your mind to shape your reality
  • Follow your purpose with courage and clarity
  • Practice kaizen: constant, never-ending self-improvement
  • Value time as your most precious resource
  • Live with discipline, service, and gratitude
